Agime does not access data on your device except for its own database and folders within internal and external storage.

The data you enter in Agime will be stored locally on your device. When you create a backup file or an export, you will have the option to store this data in other services like e. g. Dropbox. Transmission will we done by the respective services.

Agime needs the following android permissions:

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
Needed for creating export and backup files.
INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE
Used for the optional automatic creation of daily Backup files in Dropbox.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED, WAKE_LOCK
Necessary for the notification during the acquisition time, which may be configured or disabled by the user.

Contact

Data provided by the user when contacting the vendor (for example via a contact form or via e-mail) will be stored for processing the inquiry and follow-up inquiries.
